The Second National Health and Nutrition Examination Survey (McDowell et al. 1981), is a dataset concerning health and nutritional status of adults and children in the US, contains 10,351 observations and 58 variables and is stored in a CSV file called nhanes2.csv. Among these variables is an indicator variable for hypertension (highbp) and the continuous variables age and weight.
